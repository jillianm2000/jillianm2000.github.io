📬 Education Inbox Processor
📧

This feature scans your Gmail *Education label at jillian@moderngynalaska.com, extracts resource details from emails, and adds them to your library.

1
Label emails in Gmail — When you receive a resource recommendation, conference email, or course enrollment confirmation, apply the *Education label in Gmail.
2
Open Claude (Cowork/Dispatch) — Ask Claude: "Process my Education inbox and add resources to my Learning Studio."
3
Claude does the work — Claude reads the labeled emails, extracts course names, URLs, descriptions, and suggests tags, then adds each resource to your library and archives the emails.
💡 Why not automatic? Since this is a local HTML app without server access, the Gmail integration runs via Claude's AI tools in a connected session. This keeps your data private and on your device.

🔖 Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet lets you save any webpage to your Learning Studio with one click while browsing.

How to install:

1
Show your browser's bookmarks bar (Cmd+Shift+B in Chrome)
2
Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar, or right-click and "Bookmark this link"
3
While on any resource page, click the bookmarklet — it will ask for a title and tags, then add it to your Learning Studio
